What VOICE Offers

Ranging from interactive political maps to helpful instructions, VOICE offers an abundance of resources to guide users through the process of voting. With an organized dashboard that presents duties, tasks, and relevant infromation, users can easily access and learn about how to vote. There are also steps to vote in addition to a chat forum where first time voters and website VOICE users can talk with others about their political opinions and questions they have about voting so we can all be more aware!

The Problem + Our Solution

For the past 48 years, the percentage of people casting their votes from the voting-eligible population (VEP) and voting-age population (VAP) has been around only 50-60%. So, why don't people vote? According to a study held by the Pew Research Center, half of the surveyed population reported that they did not learn about the election, thinking that the wouldn't matter anyway. Many Americans don't realize the impact of their vote. By providing proper preparation and guidance, VOICE hopes to educate those who haven't tried to vote.
